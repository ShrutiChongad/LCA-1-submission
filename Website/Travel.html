Kerala, located on India's southwestern coast, is a popular tourist destination renowned for its stunning backwaters (a network of interconnected lagoons and canals), pristine beaches, lush green hill stations, rich wildlife, and unique Ayurvedic treatments, making it a perfect place for a relaxing and picturesque vacation experience

Kerala is famous especially for its ecotourism initiatives and beautiful backwaters.Kerala has unique culture and traditions coupled with its varied demography.
